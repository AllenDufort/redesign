Conclusion

This project was a great opportunity for me to apply the principles of usability and accessibility on a real-world website. By analyzing Heng's website, I identified several areas for improvement, including the layout, the color palette, the navigation bar, and overall visual design. The redesigned website addresses these issues by providing a more intuitive and user-friendly experience.

Through this project I learned about the many factors that go into responsive redesign, such as ensuring that the website works well on different devices and that the website is user-friendly for all users, including those with disabilities. I also learned the importance of creating a visual design style guide and mockups to communicate design ideas effectively amongst other develops.
